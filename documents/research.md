# Financial Reporting Tools for Presenting to Clients of Financial Advisors

Research conducted via Firecrawl MCP (February 2025). Five websites that offer or survey financial reporting tools used by financial advisors to present to clients.

---

## 1. Addepar

**URL:** https://addepar.com/wealth-management

**Summary:** Wealth management software and portfolio reporting platform for RIAs and wealth managers. Built to give a single view of client wealth and support client-facing reporting.

**Relevant features for client presentation:**
- Consolidated view of client portfolios (all accounts, asset classes, entities)
- Customized, branded reports produced in minutes
- Analytics and performance reporting to tailor insights per client
- Client portal with encrypted access and optional mobile/iPad apps
- Scenario modeling and forecasting for client conversations

**Notable quote:** "Producing reports, or other supporting materials for client meetings, requires just minutes versus a half day or longer." (WE Family Offices)

---

## 2. Nitrogen (formerly Riskalyze)

**URL:** https://nitrogenwealth.com

**Summary:** Tech platform for advisors with a strong focus on client reporting: risk profiling, portfolio analytics, and compliance-friendly communication.

**Relevant features for client presentation:**
- Tailored reports aligned to client goals, risk tolerance, and time horizon
- Automation and standardization so every client gets consistent, high-quality reports
- Visually clear reports (charts, graphs) for varying levels of financial literacy
- Optional educational content inside reports to explain concepts and metrics
- Compliance-oriented recordkeeping and data export for audits (e.g., SEC, state)

**Considerations (from Nitrogen’s own guidance):** Firm size, scalability, data security, and user experience when choosing a reporting tool.

---

## 3. Masttro

**URL:** https://masttro.com (product/overview); https://masttro.com/insights/best-wealth-reporting-software (comparison article)

**Summary:** Wealth reporting and aggregation platform aimed at RIAs and family offices with complex, multi-asset portfolios (including alternatives and illiquid assets).

**Relevant features for client presentation:**
- Global Wealth Map: single interactive view of total wealth across entities, asset classes, and geographies
- Real-time aggregation of public and private assets (e.g., private equity, real estate, collectibles)
- Alternative investment tracking (capital calls, distributions, valuations) with minimal manual reconciliation
- Large custodian integration set for accurate, up-to-date reporting
- Emphasis on clarity and “wealth in full view” for client conversations

**Context:** Their “Best Wealth Management Reporting Software” article also compares AssetBook, Schwab Advisor Portfolio Connect, Advisor360, Wove (BNY Pershing), First Rate Vantage, and PowerAdvisor Tools.

---

## 4. Morningstar Office

**URL:** https://www.morningstar.com/business/brands/wealth/products/morningstar-office

**Summary:** RIA practice and portfolio management software that combines Morningstar research with client reporting and proposal tools.

**Relevant features for client presentation:**
- Proposals and goal-based plans (e.g., via Goal Bridge)
- Portfolio performance reporting with Morningstar research and data
- Client relationship tools: account data, risk analysis, and investment research in one place
- Risk models and risk profiling to show portfolio risk vs. target allocation and recommend allocations
- Automated data and reconciliation to free time for client conversations and reporting

**Positioning:** “Combine the Morningstar insights that clients trust with the tools to grow your practice” and “meaningful conversations with clients and prospects.”

---

## 5. Advisor360

**URL:** https://www.advisor360.com (referenced in industry roundups; product details often via partner or comparison articles)

**Summary:** Connected wealth management platform that bundles reporting, workflows, and a client portal for advisor efficiency and client collaboration.

**Relevant features for client presentation (from third-party descriptions):**
- Bundle, schedule, and run fully reconciled reports
- Client portal for digital collaboration and sharing reports with clients
- Centralized advisor experience and streamlined workflows
- Data storage and reporting designed to support compliance requirements

**Best for:** Advisors who want reconciled reports, a client portal, and a single place for workflows and client communication.
